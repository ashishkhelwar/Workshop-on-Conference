How to Use the Select Editor

Click any element on any slide to inspect and edit it — live, no page reload needed.

1
Activate Select Mode
Click the Select button in the top navigation bar, or press S on your keyboard. A blue gradient bar appears at the top of the screen and your cursor becomes a crosshair.

✎ EDIT
↑ Blue bar = Select mode is ON or press S
2
Hover to Highlight an Element
Move your mouse over any text, heading, card, badge, or stat on a slide. The element you are hovering lights up with a blue dashed outline.

Click to Select & Open Inspector
