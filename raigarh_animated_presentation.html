4 Biomechanical Reasons
Calves Drown While Adults Don't

📏 Height

Calf = 0.8–1.2 m · Adult = 2.5 m. Same pool, lethal for calf only.

💪 Strength

Mud traps calf limbs. Adult mass frees it; calf exhausts itself.

🧭 Behaviour

Male calves explore more, enter water first — 70% of victims are male.

☠ Toxin Load

Calves ingest more contaminated water per kg; arsenic hits them hardest.

⚠Escape ramps must be calf-scaled (≤1:4 slope) — an adult ramp may be inaccessible to a 6-month calf.

Drowning Root Cause

Proximate Mechanism

Root Cause — Structural Hazard

Steep/vertical banks · Deep muddy bottoms trap calf limbs · Pre-monsoon shrinkage removes egress · Calves too short to self-extract · No escape ledges

Systemic Failure

Water bodies not designed for wildlife egress · No monitoring at ranked sites · Reactive not preventive · Dehardih model not replicated
